PATH 101

PATH is W&M’s system for class search, carts, and registration. This page summarizes the features you’ll actually use.

Primary & Alternatives carts

  • Everyone has a Primary Cart (top choices) and an Alternatives Cart (backup sections/courses).
  • When you add a course to the Primary Cart, open Edit Registration Options to assign a priority (First–Ninth or Auto) and choose up to two alternatives; for multi-section courses, you can choose “any other section.”
  • You can keep a personal “scratch” cart if you like, but PATH only attempts the Primary Cart during the first registration phase.

Primary & Alternatives demo • Setting priorities demo • Quick Start PDF

Priorities, auto-priority & tie-breakers

  • Assign First (highest) through Ninth, or use Auto to let PATH rank based on demand; Auto acts as a tie-breaker when you give the same number to multiple courses.
  • Co-requisites are treated as a group and attempted at the highest priority among the grouped sections.

Solving cart issues before carts close

  • Holds must be cleared or the cart won’t process.
  • Fix registration errors (pre/co-reqs, time conflicts, closed class, instructor approval) or swap to another section.
  • You can edit carts and priorities until carts close; after that, changes wait until real-time registration.

Search smarter

  • In PATH, use filters (subject, attribute, day/time) and open a course’s Details to see restrictions and co-reqs.
  • Use “any other section” as an alternate for multi-section courses to increase your chances.
